Accessing Stream

MonkeySpace is happy to offer a live stream of all Room 1 sessions. We couldn't of done this without the help from Columbia College's School of Media Arts. Please make sure to thank them!

Follow these instructions to access the stream:

  1. Open a browser and navigate to: http://63.210.98.186/
  2. Click On-going Event (right side)
  3. Log in as: User=monkey Password=123
  4. Answer questions (name, email, etc). You don't have to enter real info. After entering the info press "Thank You".
  5. Select On-going Event again: Conference stream window will be on the left. You will need to stop looped student film playback on the right. The conference screen can be made bigger by pressing the full screen mode lower right corner of the left playback screen.

You can also discuss with attendees on Jabbr in the MonkeySpace room!

Enjoy, we're happy to have you a part of the event!
